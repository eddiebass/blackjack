Instructions

You win the BlackJack if the sum of your cards is exactly 21.
When the sum of your cards is above 21, you are out of the game.
You start the game by clicking the "START GAME" button, and you will be served 2 cards per hit at the first round.
If you did not hit BlackJack in first round you will pick a card "PICK CARD" until you hit blackjack or out of the game!
